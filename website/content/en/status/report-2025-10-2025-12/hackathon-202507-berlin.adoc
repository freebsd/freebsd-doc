=== July 2025 FreeBSD Hackathon in Berlin, Germany ===

Event page: [Hackathon 202507 Berlin, Germany](https://wiki.freebsd.org/Hackathon/202507)
Date: July Saturday 12th and Sunday 13th 2025
Location: Chaos Computer Club Berlin

We had been invited to hold our two day Hackathon in the halls of the Chaos Computer Club Berlin.
The full report can be found [here](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507).

The approximately 30 participants hacked on the the following projects:
  * [Local Chatbot RAG with FreeBSD Knowledge](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Local-Chatbot-RAG-with-FreeBSD-Knowledge)
  * [Cross compiling FreeBSD on macOS](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Cross-compiling-FreeBSD-on-macOS)
  * [Importing OpenSSL 3.5](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Pierre-P-khorben)
  * [The netlinkification of PF](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Kristof-P-kp)
  * [Injecting host executable into jails](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Jan-B)
  * [Updating OpenSearch ports](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Li-Wen-H-lwhsu)
  * [Ambushing Mark Phillips with a Microphone](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Benedict-R-bcr)
  * [Patching rsync to handle extattr](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Rodrigo-O-rodrigo)
  * [Checking LICENSE files against SPDX templates](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#NN)
  * [TCP/UDP checksum offloading for bhyve](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Timo)
  * [Native FreeBSD containers with Podman](https://pad.lorenz.lu/Hackathon202507#Dave-C-dch)
  * and more.
